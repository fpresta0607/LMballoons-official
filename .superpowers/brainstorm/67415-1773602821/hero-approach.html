How should the scroll-expansion hero work on your balloon site?

The ScrollExpandMedia component starts with a smaller media window that expands to fullscreen as the user scrolls. Pick which approach fits your brand best.

Fullscreen

A: Single Hero Image

One stunning balloon image (e.g. the LED Centerpiece) expands from a small rounded window to full viewport as the user scrolls. Title text splits and slides away. Clean and focused — best for a strong first impression.

↓ scroll to cycle ↓

B: Rotating Hero Gallery

Multiple balloon images cycle through the hero on a timer or with navigation dots — each one uses the scroll-expand effect. Shows range of work immediately. More complex but showcases the full portfolio upfront.

Hero
↓ scroll reveals ↓

C: Hero Expand → Gallery Reveal

Hero image expands to fullscreen, then further scrolling reveals the services/gallery sections below with smooth fade-in animations. The scroll-expand is the gateway into the rest of the page. Most cinematic experience.
